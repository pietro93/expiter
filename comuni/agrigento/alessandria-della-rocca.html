Alessandria della Rocca is a municipality of 2.500 inhabitants located in the Agrigento province in the Italian region of Sicily in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 40 and an altitude of 533.

Alessandria della Rocca accounts for about 0.57% of the total population in the province of Agrigento and about 0.004% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Alessandria della Rocca is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location.
