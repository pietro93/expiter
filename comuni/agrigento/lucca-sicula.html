Lucca Sicula is a municipality of 1.729 inhabitants located in the Agrigento province in the Italian region of Sicily in Southern Italy. It has a population density of 93 people per km² and an altitude of 513 metres above the sea level.

Lucca Sicula accounts for about 0.40% of the total population in the province of Agrigento and about 0.00287% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Lucca Sicula is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters and long, hot and dry summers. Rainfall is mostly concentrated to the winter period.

The province of Agrigento experiences on average 95.21 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.00 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 70.32 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Lucca Sicula receives around 9.1 hours of sunshine per day on average.
