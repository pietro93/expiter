Siculiana, Sicily, Italy

Siculiana is a municipality of 4.078 inhabitants located in the Agrigento province in the Italian region of Sicily in Southern Italy.

It has a population density of 99 people per km² and an altitude of 129 metres above the sea level.

Siculiana accounts for about 0.94% of the total population in the province of Agrigento and about 0.00677% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Siculiana is classified as a Climate Zone B, which means it is one of the warmest and sunniest locations in Italy. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters and long, hot and dry summers. Rainfall is mostly concentrated to the winter period.

The province of Agrigento experiences on average 95.21 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 0.00 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 70.32 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Siculiana receives around 9.1 hours of sunshine per day on average.
