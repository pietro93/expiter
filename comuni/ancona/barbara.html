Barbara, Marche, Italy

RegionMarche

Barbara is a municipality of 1.295 inhabitants located in the Ancona province in the Italian region of Marche in Central Italy.

It has a population density of 117 people per km² and an altitude of 219 metres above the sea level.

Barbara accounts for about 0.27% of the total population in the province of Ancona and about 0.00215% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Barbara is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Ancona experiences on average 51.70 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 3.02 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 81.72 days per year. There are 19.82 foggy days throughout the year. Barbara receives around 8 hours of sunshine per day on average.
