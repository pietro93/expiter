Feltre is a municipality of 20.321 inhabitants located in the Belluno province in the Italian region of Veneto in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 204 people per km² and an altitude of 325 metres above the sea level.

Feltre accounts for about 10.01% of the total population in the province of Belluno and about 0.03372% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Feltre is classified as a Climate Zone F, which means it is one of the coldest locations in Italy. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Belluno experiences on average 56.50 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 26.50 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 106.56 days per year. There are 11.49 foggy days throughout the year. Feltre receives around 6.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
