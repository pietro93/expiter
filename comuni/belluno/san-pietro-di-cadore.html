San Pietro di Cadore is a municipality of 1.537 inhabitants located in the Belluno province in the Italian region of Veneto in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 29 and an altitude of 1.100.

San Pietro di Cadore accounts for about 0.76% of the total population in the province of Belluno and about 0.003% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

San Pietro di Cadore is classified as a Climate Zone F, which means it is one of the coldest locations in Italy.
