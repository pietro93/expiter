Pannarano, Campania, Italy

NamePannarano
ProvinceBenevento
RegionCampania
Population1.981
Density168pp/km²

Climate ZoneD

Pannarano is a municipality of 1.981 inhabitants located in the Benevento province in the Italian region of Campania in Southern Italy.

It has a population density of 168 people per km² and an altitude of 360 metres above the sea level.

Pannarano accounts for about 0.72% of the total population in the province of Benevento and about 0.00329% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Pannarano is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Benevento experiences on average 93.70 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 3.50 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 96.36 days per year. There are 9.81 foggy days throughout the year. Pannarano receives around 7.6 hours of sunshine per day on average.
