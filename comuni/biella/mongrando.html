Mongrando is a municipality of 3.680 inhabitants located in the Biella province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 223 people per km² and an altitude of 355 metres above the sea level.

Mongrando accounts for about 2.07% of the total population in the province of Biella and about 0.00611% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
