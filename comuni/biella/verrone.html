Climate Zone?

Verrone is a municipality of 1.231 inhabitants located in the Biella province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy.

It has a population density of 143 people per km² and an altitude of 277 metres above the sea level.

Verrone accounts for about 0.69% of the total population in the province of Biella and about 0.00204% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Verrone is classified as a Climate Zone , which means it is . The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Biella experiences on average 44.30 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 15.50 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 104.28 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Verrone receives around 7.2 hours of sunshine per day on average.
