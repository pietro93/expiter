Castel del Rio is a municipality of 1.203 inhabitants located in the Bologna province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 23 and an altitude of 215.

Castel del Rio accounts for about 0.12% of the total population in the province of Bologna and about 0.002% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Castel del Rio is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town.
