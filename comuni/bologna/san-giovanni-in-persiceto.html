San Giovanni in Persiceto is a municipality of 27.842 inhabitants located in the Bologna province in the Italian region of Emilia-Romagna in Northern Italy. It has a population density of 243 people per km² and an altitude of 21 metres above the sea level.

San Giovanni in Persiceto accounts for about 2.74% of the total population in the province of Bologna and about 0.04620% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

San Giovanni in Persiceto is classified as a Climate Zone E, which means it is a fairly chill town. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Bologna experiences on average 83.49 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 14.30 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 80.16 days per year. There are 25.89 foggy days throughout the year. San Giovanni in Persiceto receives around 7.8 hours of sunshine per day on average.
