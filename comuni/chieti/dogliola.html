Dogliola, Abruzzo, Italy

NameDogliola
ProvinceChieti
RegionAbruzzo

Climate ZoneD

Dogliola is a municipality of 323 inhabitants located in the Chieti province in the Italian region of Abruzzo in Southern Italy.

It has a population density of 27 people per km² and an altitude of 445 metres above the sea level.

Dogliola accounts for about 0.08% of the total population in the province of Chieti and about 0.00054% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Dogliola is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by cold winters and hot but windy summers, with somewhat frequent rainfall spread throughout the year.

The province of Chieti experiences on average 62.50 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 5.42 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 80.16 days per year. There is little to no fog throughout the year. Dogliola receives around 8.1 hours of sunshine per day on average.
