Vernante, Piedmont, Italy

Climate Zone?

Vernante is a municipality of 1.112 inhabitants located in the Cuneo province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy.

It has a population density of 18 people per km² and an altitude of 799 metres above the sea level.

Vernante accounts for about 0.19% of the total population in the province of Cuneo and about 0.00185% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Vernante is classified as a Climate Zone undefined, which means it is . The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Cuneo experiences on average 46.59 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 24.89 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year.
