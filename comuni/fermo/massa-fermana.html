Climate Zone?

Massa fermana is a municipality of 877 inhabitants located in the Fermo province in the Italian region of Marche in Central Italy.

It has a population density of 113 people per km² and an altitude of 340 metres above the sea level.

Massa fermana accounts for about 0.50% of the total population in the province of Fermo and about 0.00146% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
