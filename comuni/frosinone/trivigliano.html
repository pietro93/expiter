About Trivigliano

Trivigliano is a municipality of 1.616 inhabitants located in the Frosinone province in the Italian region of Lazio in Central Italy.

It has a population density of 128pp/km² people per km² and an altitude of 780m metres above the sea level.

Trivigliano accounts for about 0.33% of the total population in the province of Frosinone and about 0.00268% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

About Trivigliano

Trivigliano is known for its historic center, the Church of San Michele, and the scenic countryside.

It offers a blend of historical and natural beauty.

Trivigliano traces its origins to ancient Roman times.

The town thrived as an agricultural center, contributing to its cultural heritage and rural charm.
