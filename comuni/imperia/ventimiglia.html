About Ventimiglia

Ventimiglia is a municipality of 22.978 inhabitants located in the Imperia province in the Italian region of Liguria in Northern Italy.

It has a population density of 428pp/km² people per km² and an altitude of 9m metres above the sea level.

Ventimiglia accounts for about 10.75% of the total population in the province of Imperia and about 0.03813% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

About Ventimiglia

Ventimiglia showcases the Old Town, Hanbury Botanic Gardens, and the Roman Theatre.

It blends historical charm with natural beauty along the coast.

Tracing back to Roman times, Ventimiglia was a significant Roman settlement.

Over the centuries, it transformed into a vital trade center and witnessed various cultural influences.
