About Maranello

Maranello is famous for the Ferrari Museum, the Fiorano Circuit, and its association with the Ferrari automobile company.

It's a hub for automotive enthusiasts.

Maranello's transformation took place in the mid-20th century.

Enzo Ferrari founded the iconic car company, leading to the town's global recognition.
