About Villanova d'albenga

Villanova d'albenga is a municipality of 2.774 inhabitants located in the Savona province in the Italian region of Liguria in Northern Italy.

It has a population density of 175pp/km² people per km² and an altitude of 29m metres above the sea level.

Villanova d'albenga accounts for about 1.00% of the total population in the province of Savona and about 0.00460% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Villanova d'albenga is classified as a Climate Zone C, which means it is a fairly warm location. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Savona experiences on average 46.49 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 2.50 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year.

Precipitation in the form of rain or snow occurs around 75.48 days per year. Fog is rarely an issue, with little to no foggy days throughout the year.

The municipality of Villanova d'albenga enjoys approximately 7.6 hours of sunshine per day. This means that the town experiences an average of 308.22 sunny days in a year.
