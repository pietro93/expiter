Meana di susa, Piedmont, Italy

NameMeana di susa
ProvinceTorino
RegionPiedmont
Population807
Density49pp/km²
Altitude681m
Climate ZoneF

Meana di susa is a municipality of 807 inhabitants located in the Turin province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy.

It has a population density of 49 people per km² and an altitude of 681 metres above the sea level.

Meana di susa accounts for about 0.04% of the total population in the province of Turin and about 0.00134% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

Climate

Meana di susa is classified as a Climate Zone F, which means it is one of the coldest locations in Italy. The local climate is characterized by winters which are long and cold and summers which are dry and very hot. There are frequent precipitations in autumn and spring, and fog is not uncommon.

The province of Turin experiences on average 59.69 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 13.61 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year. It rains (or snows) around 87.12 days per year. There are 7.99 foggy days throughout the year. Meana di susa receives around 7.5 hours of sunshine per day on average.
