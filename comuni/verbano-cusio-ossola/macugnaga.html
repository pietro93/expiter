About Macugnaga

Macugnaga is a municipality of 526 inhabitants located in the Verbano-Cusio-Ossola province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy.

It has a population density of 5,28pp/km² people per km² and an altitude of 1.327m metres above the sea level.
