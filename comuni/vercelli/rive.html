Rive is a municipality of 425 inhabitants located in the Vercelli province in the Italian region of Piedmont in Northern Italy.

It has a population density of 45 people per km² and an altitude of 126 metres above the sea level.

Rive accounts for about 0.25% of the total population in the province of Vercelli and about 0.00071% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
