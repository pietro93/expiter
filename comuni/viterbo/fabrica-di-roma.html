About Fabrica di roma

Fabrica di roma is a municipality of 8.128 inhabitants located in the Viterbo province in the Italian region of Lazio in Central Italy.

It has a population density of 234pp/km² people per km² and an altitude of 296m metres above the sea level.

Fabrica di roma accounts for about 2.56% of the total population in the province of Viterbo and about 0.01349% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.

About Fabrica di roma

Bari is the capital of Apulia and a major commercial and cultural center. Some of its landmarks are the Basilica of San Nicola, the Castello Normanno-Svevo, the Petruzzelli Theatre, and the Pinacoteca Provinciale.

Bari also has a lively waterfront, a modern city center, and a historic core, known as Bari Vecchia.

Some of the most characteristic aspects of Bari are the cult of Saint Nicholas, the patron saint of the city, the tradition of the orecchiette pasta, and the dialect of the Barese people.

Bari was founded by the Peucetians in the 8th century BC. It was conquered by the Romans, the Byzantines, the Lombards, the Saracens, the Normans, the Swabians, the Angevins, the Aragonese, and the Bourbons.

Bari was the seat of the Kingdom of Naples, the Kingdom of the Two Sicilies, and the Kingdom of Italy. It was also a strategic port for trade and commerce in the Mediterranean.

Bari was bombed by the Allies, liberated by the Polish Army, and rebuilt after World War II. It is now a thriving city and a popular tourist destination.

Climate

Fabrica di roma is classified as a Climate Zone D, which means it is a temperate town by Italian standards. The local climate is characterized by short, mild winters with hot and somewhat windy summers and overall little precipitation.

The province of Viterbo experiences on average 72.51 days of hot temperatures (over 30°C) and 4.49 cold temperature days (<5°C) per year.

Precipitation in the form of rain or snow occurs around 87.60 days per year. There are 4.59 foggy days throughout the year.

The municipality of Fabrica di roma enjoys approximately 8.3 hours of sunshine per day. This means that the town experiences an average of 336.61 sunny days in a year.
