Climate Zone?

Vitorchiano is a municipality of 5.255 inhabitants located in the Viterbo province in the Italian region of Lazio in Central Italy.

It has a population density of 174 people per km² and an altitude of 285 metres above the sea level.

Vitorchiano accounts for about 1.66% of the total population in the province of Viterbo and about 0.00872% of the overall population of Italy as of 2022.
