Healthcare System

Italy has a universal healthcare system (Servizio Sanitario Nazionale - SSN) that provides high-quality medical care to residents. benefits from this excellent system.

  • Public hospitals and clinics available
  • Private healthcare options
  • Pharmacies on every corner
  • European health insurance reciprocity
Note: EU citizens should register with the local healthcare authority (ASL) to access public healthcare services.
