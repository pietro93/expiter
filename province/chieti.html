The province of Chieti receives on average 243 hours of sunshine per month, or 8.1 hours of sunshine per day. This is 2.97% more than the average for Italy and 1.57% more than the average for the region of Abruzzo.

Throughout the year, it rains on average 6.68 days per month, which is below average for an Italian province.

Quality of Life

Healthcare

Healthcare in Chieti is above average. For every 10k inhabitants, there are around undefined pharmacies, 10.63 general practitioners and 33.1 specialized doctors per 10k inhabitants. Average life expectancy in Chieti is very high at 82.6 years of age.

Education

Chieti has a higher-than-average percentage of high school graduates, around 67.3%; and a lower-than-average percentage of university graduates, around 23.1%. The average number of completed years of schooling for people over 25 is 10.45, which is not far from the national average of 10.44. There are 2 universities within the province.

Leisure

Chieti has somewhat decent nightlife with 2.74 bars and 4.35 restaurants per 10k inhabitants.

Crime and Safety

The province of Chieti is overall very safe for expats. As of 2021, there are an average of 2350 reported crimes per 100k inhabitants. This is 17.13% lower than the national average.

There have been around 0.5 deadly road accidents and 20.6 serious work-related injuries per 10k people in Chieti. This is respectively 7.41% less driving accidents than average and -59.69% less work accidents than average.

Car theft is reportedly 6.76% lower than average with only 65.76 cases per 100k inhabitants. Reports of house thefts are 33.94% lower than average with 115.61 cases per 100k inhabitants. Cases of robbery are uncommon with 115.61 reported cases per 100k inhabitants, about 56.37% less the national average.
