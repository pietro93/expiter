Cost of Living

The average monthly income in Gorizia is around 1757.43€, which is close to the average for Italy.

The estimated cost of living is around 1654.93€ per month for an individual or 2492.81€ per month for a family of 4.

The cost for renting a small apartment (2-3 bedrooms) in a main city area is around 770.59€ per month.

Overall, Gorizia is expensive compared to other Italian provinces.

Living in Gorizia is around 0.29% more expensive than the average of all Italian provinces.
