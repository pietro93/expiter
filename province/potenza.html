Overview

The province of Potenza is the 57th largest Italian province by population with 364.960 people, located in the Basilicata region.



The larger Potenza metropolitan area comprises 100 towns (comuni) and covers an area of 659.444 km2.



The population density is 55 inhabitants per km2, making it sparcely populated.



The male to female ratio is 1.00:1.04.



Potenza is the capital city of Basilicata, sometimes called Lucania.
