The population density is 54 inhabitants per km2, making it sparcely populated.

Cost of Living

The average monthly income in Sud Sardegna is around 1464.04€, which is lower than the average for Italy.

The estimated cost of living is around 1550.39€ per month for an individual or 2315.57€ per month for a family of 4.

The cost for renting a small apartment (2-3 bedrooms) in a main city area is around 1531.91€ per month.

Overall, Sud Sardegna is affordable compared to other Italian provinces.

Living in Sud Sardegna is around 6.04% cheaper than the average of all Italian provinces.
