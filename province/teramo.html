Overview

The province of Teramo is the 72nd largest Italian province by population with 308.052 people, located in the Abruzzo region.



The larger Teramo metropolitan area comprises 47 towns (comuni) and covers an area of 195.438 km2.


The population density is 158 inhabitants per km2, making it somewhat densely populated.


The male to female ratio is 1.00:1.05.



Teramo is the capital of the province of Teramo in the Italian region of Abruzzo.


Near the Gran Sasso and Monti della Laga, on the first Apennine foothills of northern Abruzzo, Teramo enjoys a splendid panoramic position between the highest peaks of Abruzzo and the nearby Adriatic coast.
