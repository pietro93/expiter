Overview

This page provides a data-driven overview of crime and safety in Trento, in the Trentino-Alto Adige region. Based on official 2023 statistics, Trento is moderately safe compared to other Italian provinces, ranking 44 out of 106 with a safety score of 6.5/10.

Below you will find details on thefts, violent crimes, organized crime, drug-related offenses, and accidents, along with answers to common questions about safety in Trento.

Crime and Safety

The province of Trento ranks 44 out of 106 for safety according to our data, with a safety score of 6.5. There were a total of 2695.139 official reports of crime per 100,000 inhabitants in the province in 2023. This is in line with the average number of reported crimes per 100,000 inhabitants across all Italian provinces. This suggests that the crime rate in Trento is typical for Italian provinces.
