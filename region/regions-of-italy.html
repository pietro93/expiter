The 20 Regions of Italy

Italy is divided into 20 regions, each with its own dialect, food culture and economic profile. Lombardia is the most populous, Sicilia is the largest by area, and Valle d'Aosta is the smallest by every measure. The capital, Rome, lies in Lazio.
