U.S. Consular Offices in Italy

The United States maintains several consular offices across Italy to provide a range of services to American citizens and facilitate trade and diplomatic relations. These offices are categorized by the regions they serve.

Northern Italy

U.S. Consulate General Milan

Address: Via Principe Amedeo, 2/10, 20121 Milano MI, Italy

Phone: (+39) 02-290-351

Emergency After-Hours Phone: (+39) 02-290-351

Email: uscitizensmilan@state.gov

Consular District: Liguria, Piedmont, Valle d'Aosta, Lombardy, Veneto, Trentino-Alto Adige, Friuli-Venezia Giulia, and the provinces of Parma and Piacenza in Emilia-Romagna.

U.S. Consular Agency Venice

Address: Viale Galileo Galilei, 30, 30173 Tessera, Italy

Phone: (+39) 041-541-5944

Emergency After-Hours Phone: Please contact the U.S. Consulate General in Milan

Email: uscitizensvenice@state.gov

Consular Hours: Monday through Thursday, 10:00 AM-4:00 PM by appointment only.

Central Italy

U.S. Consulate General Florence

Address: Lungarno Amerigo Vespucci, 38, 50123 Firenze, Italy

Phone: (+39) 055-266-951

Emergency After-Hours Phone: (+39) 055-266-951

Email: uscitizensflorence@state.gov

Consular District: Tuscany, Emilia-Romagna (except the Provinces of Piacenza and Parma), and the Republic of San Marino.

Southern Italy and Islands

U.S. Consulate General Naples

Address: Piazza della Repubblica, 80122 Napoli, Italy

Phone: (+39) 081-583-8111

Emergency After-Hours Phone: (+39) 081-583-8111

Email: uscitizensnaples@state.gov

Consular District: Campania, Molise, Basilicata, Puglia, Calabria, and Sicily.

U.S. Consular Agent Palermo

Address: Via Vaccarini, 1, 90143 Palermo, Italy

Phone: (+39) 091-305-857

Emergency After-Hours Phone: Please contact the U.S. Consulate General in Naples

Email: uscitizenspalermo@state.gov

Consular Hours: Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM-12:30 PM by appointment only.

Other Offices

U.S. Consular Agent Genoa

Address: Via Dante, 2, 16121 Genova, Italy

Phone: (+39) 010-584-492

Emergency After-Hours Phone: Please contact the U.S. Consulate General in Milan

Fax: (+39) 010-553-3033

Consular Hours: Monday through Thursday, 11:00 AM-3:00 PM.

U.S. Consular Agent Trieste

Address: Via Roma, 15, 34132 Trieste, Italy

Phone: (+39) 040-379-4111

Emergency After-Hours Phone: Please contact the U.S. Consulate General in Milan

Fax: (+39) 040-379-4112

Consular Hours: Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM-12:30 PM by appointment only.

These consular offices provide a range of services to American citizens, including passport issuance, emergency assistance, and notarial services. They also facilitate trade and diplomatic relations between the United States and Italy.
