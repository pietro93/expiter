Crime and Safety

The province of Viterbo ranks 30 out of 106 for safety according to our data, with a safety score of 7.5.

There were a total of 3063.627 official reports of crime per 100,000 inhabitants in the province in 2023.

This is in line with the average number of reported crimes per 100,000 inhabitants across all Italian provinces.

This suggests that the crime rate in Viterbo is typical for Italian provinces.
